Welcome to our Website

About Us

We are UMBC's college roommate finding application dedicated to helping students find compatible roommates for their college experience. Our platform aims to create a supportive and comfortable living environment by matching students based on their preferences, interests, and lifestyles. We understand the importance of having a positive roommate experience, and our goal is to make the process of finding a roommate as seamless and enjoyable as possible.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a sense of community and connection among college students by facilitating successful roommate matches. We strive to alleviate the stress and uncertainty associated with finding a roommate, ensuring that students can focus on their academic and personal growth. Through our platform, we aim to create lasting friendships, promote mutual respect, and enhance the overall college experience for students.
